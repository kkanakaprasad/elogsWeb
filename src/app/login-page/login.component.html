E-LogS: Ease of Logistics Services

is a digital system for registering, coordinating, and monitoring the resolution of user issues/suggestions. The dashboard has been developed by the Logistics Division, DPIIT to facilitate timebound resolution of issues.

Features of E-LogS:

  • Single-dashboard for submission of logistics service-related issues/suggestions
  • Promotes evidence-based decision making
  • Digitized recording, tracking, and resolution
  • Established SLAs and escalation matrices
  • Institutional knowledge repository
